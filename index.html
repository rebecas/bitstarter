FAQ

How does the collar detect a bark from my dog?
The collar use both vibration and sound sensors to discriminate each bark from other external noises like another's dogs bark or a car backfiring. When the bark collar detects both the sound from the bark and the vibration in your dog's throat it responds by sending a tweet.
How does the collar connect to wi-fi?
We use technology from electric imp. An imp is a powerful, yet tiny, hardware module that enables wi-fi connectivity empowering our devices with intelligence, customization, and convenience.
